Furry Angels Sanctuary

A dog sanctuary based in Bulgaria

Furry Angels is a home to more than 500 stray dogs. The sanctuary neuters all dogs on arrival and gets rid of parasites. Dogs are given fresh water, food and beds in a sheltered structure. Employees and volunteers help to feed and bathe them. You can support the sanctuary with direct donations, volunteering, fundraising and attending the adoption events.
